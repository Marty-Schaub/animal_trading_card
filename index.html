Gray Wolf

Under the right conditions, a wolf's howl can be heard up to 50 square miles.

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus
  • Average Length: 41-63 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 7-8 years
  • Habitat: Deserts, grasslands, forests and arctic tundras

Although wovles were once almost extinct in the lower 48 states, today they can be found in the Great Lakes, northwestern Rockies, and Southwestern United States.
